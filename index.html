Rationale

This website enables the user to see the quantitative affects of the wage gap. The data used comes from the United States Burea of Labor and lists a wide range of occupations. The graph does not account for individual occupations or industries variations in equitable treatment reguarding gender or for the earning gap present in other social groups such as race. Instead it applies the earnings gap average (women make 80 cents to a mans dollar) as a standard deviation to predict how much each industries wage gap is.
